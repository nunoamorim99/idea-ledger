Capture an idea into the Idea-Ledger using the `idea-ledger` skill, **capture mode only**.

Do the minimum and stop:
- Resolve today's date to an absolute `YYYY-MM-DD`.
- Append one line to the `📥 Inbox` section at the top of `Idea-Ledger/Backlog.md`:
  `- (YYYY-MM-DD) $ARGUMENTS`
- Do NOT assign an ID, deduplicate, rewrite, or ask questions. That happens later at triage.
- If `Idea-Ledger/Backlog.md` doesn't exist yet, scaffold it first (see the skill), then append.

This is intentionally fast and safe to run mid-work. Confirm with a one-line acknowledgement.
